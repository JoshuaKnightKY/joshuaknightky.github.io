Appalachian Trail in Georgia, Tennessee and North Carolina

Data from the Appalachian Trail Conservancy was used to overlay a trail marker over the classic, hand drawn map by Erwin Raisz, which was georeferenced in QGIS (2.14.19-Essen) using the GDAL Georeferencer.

Format:
11.69" x 8.26" (PDF)

Brewery Density of North America

Using brewery location data from The POI Factory accessed February 6, 2017 and generating 50km hexgrids in QGIS, this map shows the density of breweries across all of North America.

Format:
20" x 15" (PDF)

Cancer Death Rates by County in 2016

Data downloaded from the National Cancer Institute's State Cancer Profiles including age-adjusted rates were normalized and combined with 5-year trends to develop this interactive chloropleth map. Clicking on individual counties provides detailed information.

Format:
(CARTO)

Wildfires in 2013

Spatial wildfire occurrence data from the U.S. Forest Service Research Data Archive has been analyzed, generating an interactive map to compare percentages of total burned acreages per county to total county area in 2013. At higher levels of zoom, specific locations of wildfires become visible with more detailed information available upon clicking.

Format:
(CARTO)

Major waterways of Kentucky

Data from the National Atlas of the United States, March 2016, 2nd. edition was used to map water bodies, urbanized areas, and streams classified by Strahler ranking.

Format:
30" x 15" (PDF)

Strip & Frack

This interactive and color-coded map shows powerplants using natural gas and coal to produce energy across the U.S.

Format:
